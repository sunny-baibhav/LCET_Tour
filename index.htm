About the Trip

Welcome to the official page for the Ludhiana College Trip! Join us for an exciting adventure filled with fun and exploration.

"On February 8, 2024, our college gang is gearing up for an exhilarating journey from Ludhiana College to the sacred destinations of Shri Anandpur Sahib Gurudwara and Mata Naina Devi Temple in Himachal Pradesh. With excitement pulsing through our veins, we eagerly anticipate immersing ourselves in the rich spiritual and cultural heritage of these revered sites. Shri Anandpur Sahib Gurudwara, a bastion of Sikhism, promises to envelop us in tranquility and devotion as we pay homage to its hallowed grounds. Following this spiritual sojourn, we'll ascend to the serene heights of Mata Naina Devi Temple, nestled amidst the picturesque landscapes of Himachal Pradesh. Surrounded by breathtaking vistas, we'll partake in prayers and seek blessings from the divine mother. As we embark on this meaningful pilgrimage, we're not just embarking on a physical journey, but also a soulful exploration that binds us closer as friends and strengthens our spiritual connection. Here's to a journey filled with camaraderie, enlightenment, and cherished memories."
